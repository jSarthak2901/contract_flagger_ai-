## Overview
LexiAI is an AI-driven system that analyzes legal documents to identify risks, missing clauses, and compliance issues. It is designed to simplify contract review by converting complex legal language into structured insights and actionable recommendations.

The goal of the project is to reduce manual effort in contract analysis and make legal risk detection more accessible, especially for startups and small businesses.

---

## Problem
Legal documents are often difficult to interpret and require expert knowledge. Current challenges include:

- Time-consuming manual contract review  
- High dependency on legal professionals  
- Difficulty in identifying compliance issues  
- Risk of overlooking critical clauses  

---

## Solution
LexiAI provides an automated approach to contract analysis using NLP and AI models. The system processes legal text and highlights important insights such as:

- Risky or ambiguous clauses  
- Missing essential clauses  
- Compliance-related issues  
- Suggested improvements in simple language  

---

## Features
- Automated contract analysis  
- Clause identification and classification  
- Risk detection and compliance checking  
- Simplified explanations of legal content  
- Suggestion of improvements for better clarity  

---

## How It Works
The system follows a structured pipeline:

1. Input Processing  
   The user provides a contract in text format.

2. Text Analysis  
   The document is processed using NLP techniques to extract meaningful components.

3. Clause Understanding  
   The system identifies different types of clauses and their intent.

4. Risk Evaluation  
   It checks for missing, weak, or risky sections in the contract.

5. Output Generation  
   The system returns a summary of risks along with suggestions.
